一些翻译题

## 1

梅花位居中国十大名花之首，源于中国南方

The plum blossom, the **king** of the China's **top ten** famous flowers, **originated** in **southern** China.

隆冬时节，五颜六色的梅花不畏严寒，迎着风雪傲然绽放

**In the depths of** winter, colorful plum blossoms bloom **vibrantly amidst the** wind and snow, **unhindered by severe frost**.

梅花象征坚强、纯洁、高雅，激励人们不畏艰难，砥砺前行

The plum blossom, a symbol of **strength**, **purity** and **elegance** , motivates people to **brave hardship and forge ahead**.

自古以来：since ancient times 不朽作品：immortal works 普通大众：general public 冒着严寒：irrespective of the severe cold
